HOME

Welcome to YouServe!

Our mission is to help you get engaged in community service. On our platform you can find varying service opportunities, log your hours, write and share about your personal experiences! Not only will you be able to find volunteer opportunities at the click of a button, you will also be able to log and write about your participation for future references. We hope that by reading about other people’s involvement, you too will be encouraged to engage in social good and serve those around you. We believe that no one is too young or too old to make a difference. Together, we can change the world.

How it Works

We have created a website that we hope you find easy and enjoyable to use. At the top of each page, there are four tabs, two of which we believe are unique to our project: Volunteer Opportunities and My Personal Journal. Under ‘Volunteer Opportunities’, you will find various organizations that depend on volunteers and donations to ensure that their aims are carried out. You can also read and write reviews on the experiences that you have had in the past and reach out to others that seem to have similar interests to you. We hope that testimonies such as these will encourage others to spend their time donating and volunteering in their communities. Under ‘My Personal Journal’, users will be able to log their hours and write about their personal experiences which can be used for both personal use or future references. In order for reviews and personal journals to be updated, be sure to be logged into a personal email account! We have worked hard to create these special features and we really hope that you enjoy and are encouraged to serve those around you!
